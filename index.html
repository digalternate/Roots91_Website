About Roots91

At Roots91, we believe in bringing you snacks that are not just delicious but also wholesome. Our journey began with a simple idea: to transform the humble foxnut (makhana) into a gourmet snacking experience.

Sourced from the finest farms, our foxnuts are meticulously roasted and seasoned with unique, authentic flavors. We're passionate about healthy living and strive to offer products that are natural, gluten-free, and perfect for guilt-free indulgence.
